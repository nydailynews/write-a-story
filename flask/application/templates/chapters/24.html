Chelsea Clinton had waited to hear those words since her best friend’s father had been revealed in a leaked Access Hollywood tape to be a serial philanderer and molester of women. She had expected a call from her BFF after America’s intelligence agencies concluded that the Russians helped her father get elected. She had expected a call after her best friend’s father tweeted random conspiracy theories and claims that millions of people voted illegally. She was certain her best friend would reach out after her father, the then-President, submitted a budget with zero dollars for the National Endowment for the Arts and other causes dear to the two friends. And she was stunned when her best friend didn’t call when the vice president executed a completely legal coup against her father, the then-President.

What took you so long? Clinton said, non-judgmentally.

Well, you know, your mom...my dad...the election...the ‘lock her up’ stuff, Ivanka started. It got all complicated.
