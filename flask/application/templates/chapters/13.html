The Vice President prays away the Putin

Wednesday, June 21, 2017
Early morning

Deep State had the keys to pretty much every apartment in town — and found Perry, his pants around his ankles and Emma Slater's leotard around his neck, hanging from a hook on the back of his bedroom door.

The energy secretary was still conscious as the agent pulled out a knife and was about to cut him down.

Don't use the knife, Perry managed to sputter. Just pull me down!

Deep State put Perry in a bear hug and lifted him over the hook, relieving the pressure on his neck. Perry collapsed on the floor and untied the leotard. Rubbing his neck, he profusely thanked the stranger who had saved his life, all the more so for sparing Slater’s leotard, his cherished souvenir.

They wanted whoever found me to think it was autoerotic asyphixiation, Perry said.

They, sir? Deep State asked, keeping up the notion that he was just a Good Samaritan.

Deep State tuned out Perry’s desperate pleas for belief. Fact was, the Russians wanted Perry dead because he had signed onto Pence's plot. Putin would act again, Deep State knew, unless Mike Pence gave him a reason to stop.


Later that night

Vice President Mike Pence was on his computer in the Eisenhower Executive Office Building Googling the term pray away the gay conversion therapy online course when, suddenly, the screen went black but for the universal command prompt: C:\, with a blinking cursor. Then, he watched as the letter R appeared beside the prompt, followed by U and N. Someone, somewhere appeared to be typing remotely.

Wilhelm, Pence hollered. Get in here!

His aide entered, and Pence explained what had happened.

I was just trying to download, er, something when the entire screen went blank and this command line appeared!

What were you searching for, sir? Wilhelm asked.

Um, er, some hard facts, er, something for a bill that's being proposed, he stammered.

Of course, sir, Wilhelm said.

Startled and more than a little fearful, Pence watched as the screen filled with lines of code, thousands of them, scrolling up and up and up for about 45 seconds. When the data dump was done, these words appeared on the screen:

  • I saved Rick Perry's ass.
  • You're welcome.
  • But you still have a problem with the Russians, who will be back.
  • This should neutralize them.
  • Happy hunting,
  • Deep State.

Next, the computer fired up the White House email system, opened up a new blank email and sent the entire dump to Vladimir Putin's private email address, мужественныйПутин69 (ManlyPutin69).

Well, whatever that document was, Pence said, I just sent it to Vladimir Putin!

Trembling, Pence used the scroll bar to go back to the top and started reading:

Eyes only. Agent Moscow 2094 report. Subject: Vladimir Vladimirovich Putin.

Oh my god, Wilhelm, Pence burst out. The CIA had an agent in Moscow to get kompromat on Putin.

Agent Moscow 2094 reported that Putin hung out in a bar hidden in the basement of the Museum of the History of Corporal Punishment on Arbat Street near the Kremlin, an institution filled with torture devices from the czars and the KGB.

On 2 July, 2016, the secret bar had featured music by Sonny Pullman and the Jug Dealers — the report said Putin loves bluegrass, who knew? — with Agent Moscow 2094 undercover as violinist Irina Raskolnikova.

Pence knew the dossier was a bombshell that could stop Putin in his tracks. So he sent the Russian president a second email: I know your secret. So if you move against another Cabinet member, I will not only tell the world, but I will also tell Margitta Herstellung.

Margitta Herstellung. Silver medalist in the bantamweight division at the 1972 Olympics.

Putin will know exactly who I mean, Pence said. The real woman who should be wearing that gold.

Pence hit send. Russia’s President had been neutralized. Donald Trump’s days as President were numbered.

Great work, sir, Wilhelm said.

Thank you, Wilhelm, Pence said. Now, can you get someone to fix my computer? I do need to get back to the important search I had started.
